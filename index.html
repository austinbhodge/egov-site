Frequent Inquiries

What is e-governance?

e-governance is the concept of using information communication technologies (i.e. Internet) to govern more effectively.

Why should we change?

Our constitution was built for adaption. The purpose of a representive democracy is to allow those who are governed to choose who will govern. The problem is that an election allows the majority to be represented, but excludes minority representation. Even in the case of a 49% minority, which could represent tens of thousands of people, do we still favor a winner gets all system.

note: This is just one of the many issues that e-government could resolve.

How might this work?

There are countless ways a system like this might function, I propose a specific system, but in no way do I assert that my system is flawless or without need of enhancement. The specific nature of my system will be explained through out the website.

The foundation of the system would rely on ammending the constitution and recreating the legislative branch. The new system would allow citizens to indefinitely choose their representative(s).
